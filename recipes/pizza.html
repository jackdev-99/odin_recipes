Description

This simple, classic pizza recipe starts with an easy homemade dough and is topped with a simple tomato sauce, mozzarella, and your favorite toppings. It's a quick weeknight dinner that is infinitely customizable and always satisfying.

The dough takes about an hour to rise, but the actual assembly and baking time is less than 30 minutes, serving 4 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1/4 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water (105째F - 115째F)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers)

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water in a large bowl and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for 5-7 minutes. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour, or until doubled.
  4. Preheat oven to 450째F (230째C). Punch the dough down and roll or stretch it into a 12-inch circle.
  5. Transfer dough to a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  6. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  7. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and arrange your desired toppings.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
