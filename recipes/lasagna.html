Lasagna recipe

Return to homepage

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat pasta, and possibly one of the oldest types of pasta. A layered baked dish with pasta sheets alternating with sauces, cheeses, and often meats or vegetables. This particular recipe yields a hearty, comforting meal perfect for a family dinner.

It takes about 30 minutes of prep time and 45 minutes of baking time, serving 6-8 people easily.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic in a skillet until the beef is browned. Drain fat.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and basil. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Mix the ricotta cheese and egg in a bowl.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Spread a layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Arrange 3 noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  7. Top with a layer of meat sauce and 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat layers once more. Top with the remaining 3 noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes, or until bubbly and cheese is melted.
